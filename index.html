About me

Thank you for visiting my site. I am an assistant professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering (MAE) at The Ohio State University. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of Maryland (UMD), USA, under the supervision of Prof. Derek A. Paley in 2020. Prior to that, I did my undergrad in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Jadavpur University, India in 2015. I spent two years in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering (MAE) at Princeton University as a postdoc.

I am looking for PhD students in the area of control, dynamical systems, machine learning, and aerial robotics. Please consider sending me an email with your CV if interested.
